What are we fixing?

It’s incredibly difficult to give away money. To give effectively means to fully understand global problems and channel funds to organizations that deal with those problems. The issue is that individuals deciding what causes are important is just not productive, nor is it fair. People collectively should be involved in decisions on reshaping our world.

How are we different?

Our effectiveness is the cornerstone of our might. We don’t operate with traditional baggage like offices, administrative staff, and other overhead costs. Our structure allows us to provide continuing support for organizations that prove to make a difference.

Our Promise

We promise to always put people’s voice first, to base our decisions not on personal.

People’s Foundation currently allows anyone to be a part of the difference we make.

One time contribution of “$n” will allow us to donate on average “$n x 0.128” more to the most effective charities over the next 50 years.

FAQs

How do you create a list of causes to support?

We update the list constantly to better align with the causes people care about.
We also accept suggestions on causes that are often overlooked.

I'd like to volunteer, how can I join?

We accept applications and provide volunteer hours. Please send your resume to info@ruthlessgiving.org

How do you decide which charities to fund?
s

Charities are recommended from rigurous charity evaluators and reputable foundations that specialize in specific causes.

Can I donate publicly traded stocks, real estate, life insurance or cryptocurrency?

Yes, you can donate all of the above by contacting us at info@ruthlessgiving.org or with a via our contact form below

Do you have fees?

We do not have fees, we use a Donor Advised Fund (DAF) to facilitate our operation, and all DAF fees and money management fees are covered by the Ruthless Giving Organization. For donations made by the credit card, transactional fees will apply.

How does the People's Foundation raise money?

People's Foundation raises money from visionary individuals and institutions that share our mission, to better this world around us in the most productive way.

Can I withdraw my funds back into my bank account?

We’re afraid not. As soon as a donation receipt is provided, the contribution becomes tax exempt, and cannot be withdrawn.

Why do you believe this model works?

We strongly believe to productively impact the world we live in, we must act together through a democratic model that includes everyone in the conversation.

Is the People's Foundation a registered charity?

People's Foundation uses a Donor Advised Fund (DAF) to hold funds, which is a registered charity. In Canada we use Charitable Impact 845528827RR0001.

Is my contribution tax deductible?

Yes, your tax reciept will be emailed to you as soon as your donation is processed.

What do you do with my email?

We use your email to communicate with you about upcoming decision and we rely on your feedback on the direction in which People's Foundation moves. We do not share your email with any of the charities we fund, nor do we share your email with any 3rd party agencies.
